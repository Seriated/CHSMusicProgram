Performances

Performances Cazbah Interludes Competitions Community Events

Students in the music program participate in a variety of performances throughout the year, including school concerts, public showcases, competitions, and community events. These performances provide students with invaluable stage experience, boosting their confidence and refining their skills. The annual music showcase is a highlight, featuring soloists and ensembles demonstrating their musical talents.
