Rock Band Workshop

RBW

This workshop program is a precursor to the later year 9-12 CMPs, and is for vocalists, guitarists, bass guitarists, pianists, and drummers from years 7-8. Entry is through audition only and having intermediate knowledge on your chosen instrument is required before entry. This program gives skills and experience to students with playing in a contemporary band setting.
