Secondary Ensembles

Secondary Ensembles Vocal Ensemble Stage Band CMP RBW Jazz Orchestra Jazz Combo Instrumental Ensembles

Secondary ensembles are advanced performance groups that allow students to specialise in specific musical styles and techniques. These ensembles challenge students with complex repertoire, encourage artistic expression, and offer performance opportunities at prestigious events and competitions. Members of secondary ensembles refine their technical abilities and develop advanced musicianship while working closely with professional musicians and educators.
