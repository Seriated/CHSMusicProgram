Primary Ensembles

Primary Ensembles Symphony Orchestra Advanced Choir Intermediate Choir Intermediate Band Concert Band Training Band Guitar Ensemble String Preps

Primary ensembles are the cornerstone of the school's music program, providing students with the opportunity to develop fundamental musical skills, enhance their technical abilities, and perform in structured group settings. These ensembles cater to a range of instruments and experience levels, preparing students for advanced ensemble participation. They form the foundation of students' musical journeys, allowing them to build their confidence and develop strong ensemble discipline.
