Contemporary Music Program

CMP

The CMP groups are for years 9 to 12 only, and include vocalists, drummers, guitarists, bass guitarists & pianists. Entry to these groups is quite challenging as spots are scarce and the auditioning is very competitive. To be in CMP it is assumed you have at least an intermediate to advanced knowledge of your instrument, and are comfortable with performing for a large audience as a small band.
