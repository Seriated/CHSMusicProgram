Interludes

Interludes focus on smaller, more intimate performances, often showcasing soloists or small ensembles. These concerts are designed to provide a personal platform for students to perform in a setting that fosters a closer connection with the audience. These performances are typically hosted each term and in between yearly Cazbahs or more important dates. Interludes often only include a small portion of the overall bands & ensembles in music, with certain interludes focused on specific families of instruments, allowing students & more experienced musicians alike to practice performing on stage with a smaller audience.
