String Ensemble

String Ensemble

This ensemble was created for students who are learning to play bowed strings who may be of any age but must have mastered at least a basic level on their instruments. Students will play a wide range of repertoire, from classical works, to popular music & film music. This group is a great upgrade for students who are finding String Preps too easy.
