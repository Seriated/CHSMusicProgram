Cazbah

Cazbah is the most significant event in the school's musical calendar, bringing together all major ensembles for a large-scale concert. The event is typically divided into two parts:

  • Outdoor Festival:
    The event begins with an outdoor festival featuring live music performances from various ensembles, paired with food trucks to create a vibrant community atmosphere. This informal segment sets the tone for the evening and allows students to perform in a more relaxed environment.

  • Formal Concert:
    Following the outdoor festival, the concert moves indoors to a formal concert setting. Here, various ensembles perform structured, polished pieces, offering students the opportunity to hone their stage presence and musicality in front of a more traditional audience. This segment is often accompanied by the Cazbah Music Awards, which recognize the achievements of outstanding performers, further motivating students to excel.
